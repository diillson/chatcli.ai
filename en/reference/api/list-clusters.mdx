---
title: "List Clusters"
api: "GET /api/v1/clusters"
description: "Returns the list of Kubernetes clusters managed by the platform (federated environment)"
---

<ParamField query="status" type="string">
  Filter by status: `healthy`, `degraded`, `unreachable`
</ParamField>

<ParamField query="region" type="string">
  Filter by region (e.g., `us-east-1`, `sa-east-1`)
</ParamField>
